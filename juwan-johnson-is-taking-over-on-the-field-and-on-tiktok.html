Juwan Johnson is taking over on the field and on TikTok

 Posted on August 19, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  368 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Juwan Johnson is having a breakout season for the New Orleans Saints. After joining the team as a wide receiver after going undrafted in 2020, he made the switch to tight end in late 2021, and it has been a great move for him thus far.

After scoring four touchdowns in just 13 catches for 159 yards last season, Juwan Johnson has taken on a much bigger role in the Saints' offense this season. He has 31 catches for 349 yards, which are both third on the team behind Chris Olave and Alvin Kamara, and he also has a career-best five touchdown catches, which is a team-high.

But while Juwan Johnson’s big breakout on the field is coming this year, it has already been a couple of years since he broke out as an off-field star thanks to his TikTok account with his wife Chanen.

The couple opened their TikTok account named “Ju and Chan” in March 2020, just a month after getting married. And after taking a while to find their footing, Ju and Chan finally got their big break by creating and posting videos that give a comedic spin on the relationship between an NFL player and his wife.

Their videos have turned out to be a massive hit as they've now amassed over 2 million followers on TikTok. 

But even though Juwan Johnson has blown up into a big TikTok celebrity over the last couple of years, it is clear from his fine performances this season that he is still 100% committed to taking over on the field and becoming the top NFL player he can be.
