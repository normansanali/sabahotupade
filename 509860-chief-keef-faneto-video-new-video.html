Chief Keef &quot;Faneto&quot; Video

 Posted on August 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  220 words  |  Martina Birk

Chief Keef has finally dropped the video to "Faneto," a song he released in October 2014. The track was arguably Keef's biggest since "I Don't Like," and it remained popular well into 2015, though it's unclear why it took Sosa this long to release the video. In fact, it's unclear if Keef is still actively making music, as earlier this month, he seemingly confirmed to his fans that he had retired.  

In any case, the video is a reminder of just how huge of a song "Faneto" was, and hopefully it'll inspire fans to encourage Keef to get back in the studio. "I'm a gorilla in a fuckin' coupe, finna pull up to the zoo, n*gga," raps Keef, as his face morphs into that of a gorilla. The video then switches between violent wildlife scenes and shots of Keef sitting in a throne rolling king-sized joints. It's a warm welcome into the "Chicago Zoo." 

Last month, Keef released the video to "Superheroes," his collaboration with A$AP Rocky that landed on last year's Bang 3 album. The "Superheroes" vid had been previewed all the way back in August 2014, so it appears that Keef is sitting on some long overdue work that he's finally been able to let loose now that he has a little more free time on his hands.
