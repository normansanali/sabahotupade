Lil Nas X is baring it all in his new music video!

The 22-year-old Grammy-winning rapper teamed up with Jack Harlow for the new track “Industry Baby” and they released the sexy music video to go with it.

Click inside to watch the video!

Colton Haynes also makes an appearance in the video, playing the Head of Security who gets turned on while watching Lil Nas X‘s “Montero (Call Me By Your Name)” video.
