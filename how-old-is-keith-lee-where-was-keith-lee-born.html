How old is Keith Lee? Where was Keith Lee born?

 Posted on August 18, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  183 words  |  Martina Birk

Keith Lee (born November 8, 1984), 37years of age is an American professional wrestler currently signed to All Elite Wrestling (AEW), where he is one-half of the current AEW World Tag Team Champions with Swerve Strickland in their first reign.

He is also known for his time in WWE from 2018 to 2021 and Ring of Honor (ROH), as well as on the independent circuit with promotions such as Evolve, All American Wrestling, and Pro Wrestling Guerrilla (where he held the PWG World Championship).

Where was Keith Lee born?

Keith Lee was born in Wichita Falls, United States, Keith Lee’s birth sign is Scorpio.

In October 2017 he was declared WWN Champion for his win against Matt Riddle, He was introduced to professional wrestling by his grandmother as a child. He is romantically linked to Stephanie Bell.

He was introduced to professional wrestling at a young age by his grandmother, who was a fan herself, and credits her with inspiring him to pursue a career in professional wrestling. Lee played college football at Texas A&M University but quit to become a professional wrestler.
