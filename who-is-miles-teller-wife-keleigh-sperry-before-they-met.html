What Was Keleigh Sperry Doing Before She Met And Married Miles Teller?

 Posted on August 27, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1133 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Miles Teller has an impressive portfolio when it comes to all the movies he has acted in since he came into the limelight in 2010 where he made his featured film debut in a movie named Rabbit Hole.

Apart from that, the actor is known for being eye candy to many ladies due to his attractive physical feature, but only one woman caught his eye and he decided to put a ring on it and make it official with her in 2019. That woman is Keleigh Sperry.

While Miles Teller and Keleigh Sperry were on a Safari in Africa, he decided to propose to her, he told her, "This was the first day that I asked you to be my girlfriend, and today was the last day you woke up as my girlfriend."

According to People, of course, Keleigh said yes, and the rest is history.

So before all the bliss of the beautiful dreamlike relationship between the famous Top Gun actor Miles and his wife, Keleigh, was established and sealed, what was Keleigh Sperry doing with her life before she met her husband Miles Teller?

8 Who Is Keleigh Sperry?

Let's start by pronouncing her name right, Kelly. Keleigh Sperry was born in Orange County, California USA on October 16th, 1992 to her father, Rand Sperry, and her mother Rosemary White Sperry. According to Biographied, Keleigh's father founded a commercial real estate brand known as Sperry Van Ness. Keleigh's father Rand also holds the title of the chief executive officer at Sperry Equities which is a commercial real estate investment and management company.

It seems like while growing up, the gorgeous 30-year-old Keleigh had a good upbringing. She has 5 older siblings, 3 sisters and 2 brothers, and they all seem to have a healthy relationship with each other.

7 What Did Keleigh Sperry Do For A Living Before Meeting Miles?

Keleigh was interested in acting ever since she was little, but she picked up modeling as a career in the long run. In 2011, Keleigh signed a contract with One Management and modeled for them for 7 years and later moved over to LA models and Model Mayhem. So far, Keleigh has been featured in music videos for Opeth's 'The Devil's Orchard', and together with her husband, they were featured in Taylor Swift's 'I bet you think about me.' Also, Keleigh has acted in two short films one of them being 'Dance' and she starred as Bliss.

6 Keleigh Sperry Has A Large Social Media Following

Keleigh has a large following on Instagram standing at 749,000 followers but still growing. Keleigh has interesting posts on her page, from modeling pictures to vacation pictures.

Also, she loves to post her husband, friends, and family, on there as well. At this rate, we might as well just call her a social media influencer because she's also working with so many brands.

5 This Is Why Keleigh And Her Dad Are Super Close

Keleigh has a great relationship with her father. If they're not helping the needy together they are always doing something beneficial for society. On her Instagram post, she captioned an emotional, loving birthday wish on her father's birthday and many people found that message to be sweet.

4 Keleigh Sperry Loves Helping The Less Fortunate

We just love how kind and generous Keleigh and her father Rand are. She has been raised so well. They took a trip down to Uganda in Africa to help the less fortunate in society. Keleigh partnered with Wells Of Life and decided to lend a helping hand. During an interview with the Wells Of Life production, Keleigh said, "I came to Uganda with my father, He told me at lunch one day that he would be coming on this trip and it sparked a fire in me that I needed to be here and come into this with him."

3 Where Did Keleigh And Miles Meet?

According to Elle Australia, Miles and Keleigh met in 2013 at a party a band called The Black Keys had organized. They both had mutual friends.

During an interview with Vogue, Miles said on that night Keleigh had caught his eyes, and they talked. "I didn't think it went well, but after a few more attempts, I got her to dance with me, and a week later, we went on a date. After we met, I knew she was the one." Miles recalled.

Keleigh on the other hand thought that their talk went well, she said, "He was charming and charismatic. I adored him from the first moment we met."

Miles and Keleigh dated for 4 years until Miles decide to engage Keleigh.

2 When Did Miles And Sperry Get Engaged?

Miles Teller and Keleigh Sperry got engaged while on a safari in South Africa on August 20th, 2017. During an interview with People, the couple revealed details of what happened during the process of Miles proposing to Keleigh. As we mentioned earlier the proposal went well.

Sperry thought the proposal was perfect and she said, "I'll never get the image out of my head of him down on one knee with a tear in his eye. He really thought about every detail and was so nervous!"

Later that same year, the pair attended the premiere of the film, 'Only The Brave' as a newly engaged couple. You could tell that they were so happy because they couldn't stop smiling.

1 This Is When Miles Teller And Keleigh Got Married

The long-term couple Miles and Keleigh wed just about two years after their romantic engagement. Their wedding was held in Maui, Hawaii. According to Us Weekly, Miles had a hard time not being involved in the wedding preparation, but he would 'Let her take the rein on a lot of things.'
