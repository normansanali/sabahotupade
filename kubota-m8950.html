Kubota M8950 Compact Utility Tractor is equipped with a Yanmar 3TN84RJ diesel 3-cylinder liquid-cooled 87.3 ci [1.4 L] engine, which delivers 33 hp (24.6 kW) power at rated rpm 3200.

Kubota M8950 Compact Utility Tractor featured a Yanmar 1.4L 3-cyl diesel engine, 4×4 MFWD 4WD chassis, Power steering, differential hydraulic wet disc brakes, hydrostatic transmission, and infinite forward and reverse gears.

Kubota M8950 Compact Utility tractor attachments include a mowing deck, front-end loader, and backhoe.
