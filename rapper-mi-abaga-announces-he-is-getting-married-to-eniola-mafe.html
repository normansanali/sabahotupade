Rapper MI Abaga Announces He is Getting Married to Eniola Mafe

 Posted on August 26, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  173 words  |  Larita Shotwell

MI Abaga is set to join the table of men after he proudly announced on Sunday, April 3 that he’s going to get married soon.

In the post sighted by www.Ghgossip.com, the Nigerian rapper whose stage name is decoded as Mr. Incredible said he’s spending the rest of his life with Eniola Mafe and asked fans to support him on their new journey with prayers.

He however fell short in announcing the specific date he’s going to open this important chapter in his life. His post read;

I’m a private person. But it’s time to share a little part of my joy with you. @eniolamafe and I met in Dec 2020 and straight away I knew I had found the one.

I am excited to announce that we are getting married this year.. and we ask for your support and blessings as we embark on this journey together. ♥️

Eniola is the founder of New Nigeria, an organization that was formed in the wake of the #Endsars protests across the country.
