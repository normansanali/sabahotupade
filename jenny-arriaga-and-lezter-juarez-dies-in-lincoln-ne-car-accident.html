Jenny Arriaga and Lezter Juarez Dies in Lincoln NE, Car Accident

 Posted on August 24, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  350 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

In Memoriam: Jenny Arriaga and Lezter Juarez – Lives Remembered

It is with heavy hearts that we acknowledge the tragic loss of two lives in a devastating three-vehicle accident near North Platte. Jenny Arriaga-Martinez, 25 years old, and Lezter Juarez, 27 years old, both from Lincoln, Nebraska, lost their lives in the incident.

Details of the Accident:
The accident occurred on a Friday afternoon when the westbound vehicle, driven by Lezter Juarez, lost control, crossed the median, and collided with an eastbound Honda SUV and a semi-truck. The impact resulted in the ejection of both Jenny Arriaga-Martinez and Lezter Juarez from the vehicle, and they were pronounced deceased at the scene.

Our Deepest Condolences:
In the wake of this heartbreaking event, our thoughts and prayers go out to the families and friends of Jenny Arriaga-Martinez and Lezter Juarez. The loss of two vibrant lives is an immeasurable tragedy, and we share in the grief of those who knew and loved them.

Injuries to Other Occupants:
Four occupants of the Honda SUV were seriously injured in the accident and were transported to Great Plains Health in North Platte, with three of them subsequently transferred to UNMC. We extend our hopes for their swift and complete recovery, and our hearts go out to their families as they navigate this challenging time.

Winter Driving Conditions:
The Nebraska State Patrol emphasizes the importance of exercising caution during winter driving conditions. It is crucial for all motorists to reduce speeds and remain vigilant on the roads. Weather-related challenges can significantly impact road safety, and staying informed about current conditions is paramount.
