Zanab Jaffrey - Age, Family, Bio

 Posted on September 8, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  111 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Reality star who is known for being on Netflix's reality show Love Is Blind season 3. Her Instagram account zanabjaffrey has earned more than 470,000 followers.

Before Fame

She graduated from The University of Texas at Arlington. 

Trivia

She has been a flight attendant and real estate agent. Her zanabjaffrey TikTok channel has built a wide following of fans, and she uses the page as a place to post clips from her life, as well as the occasional dance video. A handful of the videos on her channel have received millions of views.

Family Life

She has two dogs and a cat. 

Associated With

She worked alongside Vanessa Lachey, Raven Ross and Nick Lachey on Love is Blind.
