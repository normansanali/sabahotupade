Battle for the Cutest Dragon Pokemon~

 Posted on August 29, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  373 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill
You're giving me too many of my favourite options >_<

Who has the cutest design?
Ugh, I could be here for weeks squealing over them. Haxorus, Giratina, Sazandra, and Dragonair, in some order.

Meanwhile, Kyuren is just ugly (sorry, Kyurem lovers =_=), Zekrom is too "cool-looking" to really be cute, and Reshiram looks like some sort of transvestite.

Garchomp... I'm not sure what to think. It's cute, but at the same time it's not cute. I dunno.

Who is the most poised/graceful in battle?
Probably Giratina. The rest of them can't twist around in the sky making beautiful curlicues in the air.

Who has the best charm?
My mind immediately turns to Garchomp wearing a fedora, like she does in Drakon's The Life of the Legendaries. I can't say no to that.

Who has the cutest pre-evolution?
Like I said, Axew looks like it has a back problem, and Deino looks like some hipster. Gible, on the other hand, looks like some sort of mutant puffer fish. The only one left there is Dratini.

Which one would you be more comfortable with beside you in bed/at the foot of your bed while you are asleep?
Well, again, Haxorus isn't exactly cuddling material. Depending on how small they were, Dragonair or Giratina would both be wrapped around me, and Garchomp would probably be at the foot of the bed anyway. Of course, it might crawl in and invade the covers.

Reshiram would probably have the body structure of an old lady, so I definitely would not feel comfortable being near that. Zekrom... might work. Kyurem is like a T-rex in that regard. No thanks.

Who is the most friendly?
This is a toughie. Out of Dragonair, Garchomp, Haxorus, Giratina, and Sazandra, they can all be equal.

Who is the most useful in battle?
Haxorus was my first ever sweeper, so some of the bias goes on him. It's also nice that it's one of the only useful Dragon types that doesn't have a 4x weakness to Ice.

Sazandra is nice too, in that regard. A Dragon-type pseudo with no 4x weaknesses is bound to have some heads scratching, but I'll love it to death

Who is your favorite?
The choice is too hard >_<
