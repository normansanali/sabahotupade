Nancy Stafford – Bio (Age)

Raised as a Southern Baptist, the actress is a practicing Christian. She is a product of Ft. Lauderdale High School and the University of Florida from where she earned a Bachelor of Arts in Journalism.

Stafford also appeared in the ABC series Companions from 1986 to 1987 and later as Michelle Thomas in the forensic drama television series, Matlock. Her role in the project lasted from 1987 until 1992 when she withdrew from the series following the relocation of her filming.
