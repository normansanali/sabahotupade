Kendall Jenner, Rihanna &amp; More Stars Looking Sexy In Orange

 Posted on July 3, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  406 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

There is no better time to wear orange than in autumn! But if you're worried about looking like a Jack-O'-Lantern, let celebs like Kendall Jenner, Rihanna, and more inspire you to wear orange boldly and proudly.

Fall is finally here, and with it, the perfect time to wear one intimidating color of the rainbow: orange! If you’re feeling out of your realm trying to don the hue that’s become synonymous with Halloween for fear of turning into a pumpkin in the process, don’t worry. Let these celebs show you how to wear orange and look sexy and confident in style!

One star who has always been bold with her fashion choices is none other than Rihanna. The “Umbrella” songstress, 32, has rocked some amazing looks, including this bright orange ensemble at the February 2020 Fenty pop-up! She layered the winter look with a long jacket over her orange sweater dress. Ever the fashionista, the mogul also added layers of necklaces to her look and bold statement earrings. Rihanna absolutely sizzled, turning the pavement into her own personal catwalk.

More recently, Jordyn Woods and her new BF, Karl-Anthony Towns, decided to match their outfits for date night. The love birds were spotted out at Nobu in Malibu on the evening of October 6. The model, 23, rocked a sheer orange dress, and matched her look with a purse that complimented her ensemble. For his part, the Minnesota Timberwolves basketball star, 24, wore an orange T-shirt with a black vest and black pants. Following safety precautions amid the coronavirus pandemic, both wore face masks to respect the health and safety of their fellow restaurant patrons.

Finally, if there’s one star who can work practically any color it’s Kendall Jenner! The supermodel, 24, wowed onlookers at the 2019 Met Gala, wearing this fabulous Versace dress. Kendall was a total stunner in the look, proving that fans can fashion orange for formal events as well as casual. The gown was a true work of art, and Kendall pulled off the often tricky color with total ease.

Orange is in and it’s the perfect time to don the color. If you’re feeling hesitant you’ll look more like a jack-o’-lantern than a person, shed the fear! These celebs and more dazzled in the quintessential autumn color, putting their own spin on it with classic detail and modern cuts.
