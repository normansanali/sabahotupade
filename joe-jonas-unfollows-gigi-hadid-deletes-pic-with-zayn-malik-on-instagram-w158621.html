Joe Jonas Unfollows Gigi Hadid, Deletes Pic With Zayn Malik on Instagram

 Posted on August 21, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  254 words  |  Martina Birk

There will be no cake by the ocean for this trio anytime soon. Joe Jonas has wiped his social media history clean of Zayn Malik and his ex-girlfriend Gigi Hadid after Us Weekly exclusively revealed this past month that the two are dating.

The DNCE band member, 26, removed Hadid and Malik from his “Following” list on Twitter. Taking it one step further, Jonas also deleted a pic with Malik posted to his Instagram and Twitter accounts this past June. The now-deleted snap featured the two pop singers at the Valentino show in Paris on June 24, alongside Kanye West. At the time, Jonas captioned the snap, “#SQUAD,” though the friendship appears to have disassembled.

The Jonas brother and Hadid, 20, split earlier this month, and it didn’t take long for the Victoria’s Secret rookie to move on. This past weekend, Hadid and Malik were photographed leaving West Hollywood hot spot, The Nice Guy, together at 2 a.m., hand in hand.

Us Weekly exclusively confirmed on Nov. 23, that the model and former One Direction bandmate are dating. “They seem equally super into each other, texting each other all the time,” a source told Us at the time. “They’ve only been hanging out a couple weeks.”
