Meghan Markle Wears $268 Club Monaco Dress, &amp; It's Not Sold Out Yet

 Posted on September 7, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  309 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Meghan Markle has most definitely mastered the art of the quick and subtle outfit change. After she caused Dion Lee's website to crash from wearing a much lusted after navy dress, Meghan swiftly changed into a black Club Monaco dress, which is surprisingly affordable and still available.

Related Story

While Dion Lee's Folded Sail dress is currently available to pre-order for $990, for her second look of the day Meghan chose Club Monaco's Miguellina dress, which retails for just $268.

Meghan changed her outfit ahead of her visit to the beach in Melbourne, opting for the sleek black dress with gold buttons at the left shoulder and on the outer left side. Per Meghan's Mirror, the Duchess wore a pair of black Rothy's flats with the outfit, because walking on the sand in heels would've been a total nightmare:

This isn't the first time that Meghan has worn a Club Monaco style, either. Back in August 2018, Meghan wore Club Monaco's Shoanah Dress dress when she attended Charlie van Straubenzee's wedding with Prince Harry. Sadly, that dress sold out within 48 hours of Meghan wearing it, so it's worth acting fast if you're a fan of her royal tour Miguellina dress.

Meghan and Harry's first royal tour is already off to a busy start, with the pair due to visit New Zealand, Fiji, Australia, and the Kingdom of Tonga in the coming weeks. Fans have been understandably excited to greet the couple, following the news that they're expecting their first child together in Spring 2019.
