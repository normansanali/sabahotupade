Gema Zamprogna is a Canadian actress who rose to fame by her role as Felicity King on Road to Avonlea (1990-1996) and Mackie “Mac” Daniels in 1991 film The Challengers. Zamprogna is a registered dance teacher at the Royal Academy of Dance (RAD). She participated with many symphonies as a dance soloist like Florida Symphonic Pops in Boca Raton, Florida.
