Street Fighter 6 Juri Combo / Move List (Character Guide)

 Posted on July 21, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  311 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Juri is focused around the Taekwondo/Feng Shui Engine, and uses the Feng Shui Engine Type Alpha to smash opponents with ki-powered kicks. She possesses a Fuha stock system in which she may collect surrounding ki and utilize it to better boost following strikes.

In the World Tour mode, you will see Juri throughout the campaign but will only become accessible as a Master to enroll under after finishing the game thoroughly.
