It Is My Birthday But I Will Celebrate You Seun Kuti Tells Wife As He Clocks 38

 Posted on August 7, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  228 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Controversial Nigerian musician and activist, Seun Kuti has penned a touching note to his wife, Yetunde George Ademiluyi as he clocks 38 today, January 11.

Celebrating this special day of his life, Seun Kuti eulogized and appreciated Yetunde for being a gift to him.

According to Seun, his wife grabs his thoughts and lifts his spirit and although it is his birthday, he has decided to celebrate her.

Sharing a loved up photo of him and his wife on his Instagram page, he wrote;

‘Happy bday to me! Born on this day 1983. Life is all about the people we meet along the way that help us become better. Today is my birthday but I’m gonna write a little message to my own special gift @chefyeide.

Today might be my birthday but I will celebrate you, the glue that makes broken China anew. She dey the trenches with the troops to empower the group.

The main pepper inside this soup! She grabs my thoughts and lifts my spirits and a little girl to remind me that the work can never finish. Thanks for being ‘ a Gift ‘ #getthesax. #8338’
