Shalini Kandukuri Wiki/Biography

Born on 27 September 1989, Shalini Kandukuri is 30 Years Old as in 2020. She was born and raised by a middle-class Hindu family in Kandukur, Andhra Pradesh, and holds the Indian Nationality.

She hails from a family of doctors, as both her parents belong to that profession.

She completed her early schooling from St. Mary’s High School in Hyderabad and after that, she enrolled herself at the University of London from where she completed her masters of business administration in management and human resources.

Since childhood, she was inclined towards the corporate world and wanted to be a part of it. So she decided to pursue her graduate course in MBA to fulfill her dream of being a corporate professional.
